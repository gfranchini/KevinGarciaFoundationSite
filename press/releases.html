The Kevin Garcia Foundation Changes the Life of a Two Year Old Girl with the Gift of Sight

The KG Foundation Presented IKF WonderFund yesterday with a Generous Donation To Fund the Surgery.

March 1, 2016 (Miami, FL)- South Florida based charitable foundation, The Kevin Garcia Foundation presented IKF WonderFund yesterday with a donation to help fund the necessary surgery needed to give a 2 year-old girl from Brazil the gift of sight for the first time in her life. The two year-old named Nicolly Beatriz Laurenco was born with Glaucoma, a condition that kept her unable to see since birth.

The generous donation from The Kevin Garcia Foundation in conjunction with Dr. Carlos Fiallo, D.M.D., came just as time was of the essence.
